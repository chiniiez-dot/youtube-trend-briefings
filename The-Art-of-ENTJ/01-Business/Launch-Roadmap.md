# Launch Roadmap — zero to $30k, with gates

The plan of record. Five phases, each with a **goal**, the **work**, and a **gate** — a measurable condition you must clear before advancing. Gates keep you honest: they replace "am I doing well?" anxiety with a yes/no answer. Timings assume the [[Financial-Model|base-case]] cadence of 2–3 videos/week.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (Weeks 0–2)

**Goal:** everything is ready so that once you start, you never stop to set up.

- Lock the brand assets from the [[Brand-Bible]]: channel name, banner, the 5 thumbnail templates (A–E), the fixed voice profile, the color/type system.
- Build the [[Production-Stack]]: pick and pay for the minimum tool set; produce one full test video end-to-end and throw it away. The point is to debug the pipeline, not to publish.
- Fill the [[Video-Backlog]] to at least **20 titles** so you're never staring at a blank page.
- Set up channel, [monetization-ready settings](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/72857), an affiliate account, and a simple email waitlist / community shell (even empty).
- **Gate:** one test video produced start-to-finish, 20 titles banked, cadence chosen and written down.

## Phase 1 — Prove the concept (Months 1–3)

**Goal:** find out if the format resonates, before optimizing anything.

- Publish on your fixed cadence, no exceptions. **Target: 12–24 videos in this window.**
- Run the [[Packaging-Playbook|three-concept]] title/thumbnail discipline on every upload.
- Do **not** obsess over subscriber count. Watch two numbers only: **click-through rate** and **average view duration** (see [[Analytics-and-KPIs]]).
- Start [[Analytics-and-KPIs|performance logging]] from video #1.
- **Gate:** ≥ 30 videos published OR a clear signal — at least one video with CTR > 5% *and* AVD > 40%. If you have neither after 30 videos, run a hard format review before continuing (don't quit — diagnose).

## Phase 2 — Reach monetization (Months 3–8)

**Goal:** clear the Partner Program and turn on the first two revenue streams.

- Keep cadence. Double down on the topic *types* that overperformed in Phase 1 (the monthly review tells you which).
- Push toward **1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours**. Long-form (8–15 min) accrues watch hours fastest; consider a few Shorts as a subscriber on-ramp, not the main act.
- Apply to the [Partner Program](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/72851) the moment you qualify.
- Launch **affiliates** (Stream 4) and stand up the **product waitlist** now, even pre-launch.
- **Gate:** accepted into the Partner Program; AdSense live; affiliate links earning something, however small.

## Phase 3 — Build the business (Months 8–14)

**Goal:** stack sponsorships and the owned product on top of ads. This is where revenue outgrows views.

- **Sponsorships (Stream 2):** with an engaged audience, respond to inbound and/or pitch via marketplaces. One integration/month to start. Protect the voice — decline hyped reads.
- **Product (Stream 3):** launch the flagship **paid community** at $15–30/mo and/or **the Playbook**. Convert the waitlist first. This is the line that bends the revenue curve.
- Begin **buying back time** — outsource editing first (see [[Financial-Model]]) so you can raise output or quality.
- **Gate:** blended revenue ≥ $8k/month (the [[Financial-Model|conservative scenario]]); at least one paying product exists with real members.

## Phase 4 — Scale to $30k (Months 14–24)

**Goal:** grow views to ~1M/month and mature all five streams.

- Raise cadence or production value with the outsourced pipeline; keep quality control non-negotiable (see [[Publishing-Checklist]]).
- Grow the community toward **~500 paying members** — the single biggest lever to $30k.
- Increase sponsorship frequency to 2–3/month at rising CPMs as the audience grows.
- Run the [[Analytics-and-KPIs|monthly review]] religiously; the Brand Bible's continuous-improvement loop is now your operating system.
- Consider the second channel or newsletter only once this one is stable.
- **Gate:** blended revenue ≈ $30k/month, sustained across two consecutive months (not a one-video spike).

---

## The rule that governs every phase

**Never break cadence to chase a phase gate faster.** Consistency compounds; sprints followed by silence do not. If you must choose between one more video this week and one perfect video next month, publish this week. Speed of iteration beats polish in the build years — the polish is guaranteed by the [[Publishing-Checklist]], the growth is guaranteed by showing up.

## The two failure modes to watch

1. **Quitting in the valley** (Months 2–8, flat views). The gates exist so you can see progress the vanity metrics hide. Trust them.
2. **Monetizing too aggressively, too early.** Selling before trust is built poisons retention (brand rule #5, the honesty gate). Ads and affiliates are fine early; hard product pushes wait until Phase 3.
