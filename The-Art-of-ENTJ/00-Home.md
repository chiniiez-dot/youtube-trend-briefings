# The Art of ENTJ — Operating Vault

The single source of truth for the channel: brand, business, content, production, and operations. Every script, thumbnail, upload, and financial decision checks against these documents.

> **The one-line goal:** build a faceless YouTube channel in the strategy-and-power lane to **$30,000/month in blended revenue** within 18–24 months, then hold it.

Read [[01-Business/Reality-Check|the Reality Check]] first. It tells you the truth about the $30k number before you spend a dollar or an hour.

## How to use this vault

1. **New here?** Read [[01-Business/Reality-Check|Reality Check]] → [[01-Business/Launch-Roadmap|Launch Roadmap]] → [[00-Brand/Brand-Bible|Brand Bible]].
2. **Making a video?** Duplicate [[_templates/Video-Production-Note]], pull a title from [[02-Content/Video-Backlog]], follow [[03-Production/Script-Template]], ship through [[03-Production/Publishing-Checklist]].
3. **Reviewing the month?** Go to [[04-Operations/Analytics-and-KPIs]] and run the monthly review from the Brand Bible.
