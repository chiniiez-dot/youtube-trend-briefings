# Publishing Checklist — the pre-flight gate

Run this before any video ships. **If any item fails, the video does not publish.** This is the Brand Bible's quality-control gate plus the upload package. Consistency is the moat; this checklist is how the moat gets defended when you're tired and want to just hit publish.

## Quality control (from the Brand Bible)

- [ ] **Voice** — narration follows the voice rules; no hype crept in.
- [ ] **Hook** — curiosity gap open within the first 15 seconds.
- [ ] **Payoff** — the video delivers the title's promise (honesty gate).
- [ ] **Application** — it answers "so what do I do."
- [ ] **Look** — near-black, gold used sparingly, **no red**, motion slow and controlled.
- [ ] **Thumbnail** — legible at 210×118px, timestamp zone (bottom-right 220×90) clear, one idea.
- [ ] **Title** — front-loaded tension, under 55 characters, honest.
- [ ] **Audio** — levels consistent, no clipping, pacing matches the voice profile.

## Packaging (from the Playbook)

- [ ] Three concepts were built and scored; the winner cleared the payoff-honesty gate.
- [ ] Winning concept logged (safe / tension / wildcard) in the [[Video-Production-Note]].
- [ ] Thumbnail uses one of the locked A–E templates and the signature visual language.

## Upload package

- [ ] **Title** finalized (40–55 chars).
- [ ] **Description** — one-paragraph hook summary, chapters, affiliate links, product/waitlist link, relevant playlists.
- [ ] **Tags / keywords** — from research, not guesses.
- [ ] **Chapters** — timestamps set (helps retention + search).
- [ ] **End screen** — points to the next logical video / series.
- [ ] **Pinned comment** — a sharp one-liner that invites discussion + the affiliate/product link.
- [ ] **Playlist / series** — added to the correct series playlist.
- [ ] **Thumbnail** — uploaded, checked at mobile size.
- [ ] **Monetization** — enabled, mid-roll ad placements set for 8+ min videos.
- [ ] **Schedule** — set to publish on the fixed cadence day/time, not "whenever it's ready."

## Post-publish (within 48h)

- [ ] Log baseline metrics in [[Analytics-and-KPIs]]: CTR @48h, AVD, retention shape, traffic source.
- [ ] Reply to early comments (first-hour engagement signals matter).
- [ ] Note any obvious retention cliff for the monthly review.

## The one rule this checklist enforces

**A video that fails the payoff-honesty gate is killed, no matter how good its numbers might be.** Short-term clickbait overperformance poisons long-term retention and trust — and trust is the entire funnel to the [[Monetization-Model|product line]] that gets you to $30k. Protecting the honesty gate *is* protecting the business.
